What kind of variable is species? And bill length? Use the class function and don’t forget your $ unless using the pipe operator!

## [1] "factor"

## [1] "numeric"
#OR

  class()
## [1] "factor"
penguins$bill_length_mm %>% 
  class()
## [1] "numeric"

Filter the gentoo and adelie species, and put it in a new dataset called two_species.

two_species <- filter(penguins, species == "Adelie" | species == "Gentoo")

#OR 

two_species <- penguins %>% 
  filter(species == "Adelie" | species == "Gentoo")

Make a box plot using your new two_species set looking at the differences between bill length of the two species of penguin. Add color based on the two species!

boxplot(bill_length_mm ~ species, data = two_species)

Run a t-test and a linear regression to see if there is a significant difference between the two species bill length averages. Hint use bill_length_mm for your dependent variable and species as your independent variable.
